EWM concepts demonstrated

Every tool is grounded in real SAP Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) logic — not just terminology.

Storage Type Search Sequence
EWM evaluates storage types in priority order at putaway. LG01 (Zone A) checked first — first match wins. Configured in SPRO.
Transfer Order (TO)
EWM's internal movement instruction — source bin to destination bin, with material, quantity, batch, and handling unit. Confirmed via RF scanner.
Quality Management (QM) Hold
Damaged stock bypasses normal putaway and goes to LG09 (Quality Inspection). QA32 usage decision required before release.
FEFO (First Expiry First Out)
Near-expiry stock routed to LG08 and picked before standard stock regardless of receipt date. Mandatory in pharma regulated environments.
Batch Traceability
Batch tracked from GR through TO, bin determination, and confirmation. Required for pharma compliance and recall readiness.
OTIF and Fill Rate
On Time In Full (OTIF) and order fill rate are the primary KPIs on every supply chain analyst dashboard. Tracked monthly against targets.
